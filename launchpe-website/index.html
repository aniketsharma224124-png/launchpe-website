The flow

From URL to
launch-ready in minutes

Five steps. Entirely automated. You only need to copy-paste and hit post.

01
Paste your product URL
That's literally it. No signup form, no brief, no onboarding questionnaire. Drop the URL of your homepage, landing page, or app.
⏱ Takes 5 seconds
02
AI reads and understands your product
We scrape your website and send the content through Claude AI. It identifies your product category, target audience, core problem solved, and key differentiators — the raw material for every piece of content.
⏱ ~30 seconds
03
Communities and platforms are mapped
Based on your product context, we recommend the 15 most relevant communities across Reddit, LinkedIn, and Twitter — with an audience fit score and a brief on each community's rules so you know what's allowed.
⏱ Instant
04
20 ready-to-post pieces are generated
Reddit story post, Reddit resource post, Reddit question post. LinkedIn founder story, LinkedIn feature post, LinkedIn hot-take. Twitter thread and 3 standalone tweets. WhatsApp group message. Product Hunt tagline + first comment. All in the right tone for each platform.
⏱ ~2 minutes
05
You get a 10-day launch calendar
Not just "post on Monday." Specific posts, specific communities, specific times — based on peak engagement windows for each platform. Download it, follow it, adjust as you learn what's working.
⏱ Included with every plan
